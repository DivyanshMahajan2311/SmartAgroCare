About SmartAgroCare

SmartAgroCare is a unified smart-agriculture platform designed to help farmers make informed decisions using machine learning and deep learning. It integrates three professional modules: Crop Recommendation, Fertilizer Recommendation, and a 7-Day Weather Forecast.

ðŸŒ± Crop Recommendation

Based on soil nutrients (N-P-K), temperature, humidity, pH and rainfall, the system predicts the best-suited crop for your field and the season to grow it. It uses a trained ML model for high accuracy and reliable decisions.

Given the crop name and soil nutrient information, the system recommends the most appropriate fertilizer to improve soil health, maintain balance and increase yield while reducing unnecessary cost and wastage.
